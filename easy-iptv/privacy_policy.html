Privacy Policy — Easy IPTV

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Easy IPTV is designed to respect your privacy. In short: we do not collect any data, and we do not host any content. Easy IPTV is a media player for an IPTV service that you provide yourself.

Data we collect

None. Easy IPTV has no user accounts and no login. We do not operate any servers that receive your data, and we do not use any analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs.

Your provider details

The credentials and URLs of the IPTV provider you add (Xtream Codes account or M3U playlist) are stored on your device — and, if you have iCloud enabled, in your own private iCloud key-value storage. They are never sent to us. They are used only to connect your device directly to the provider you configured.

Services your device contacts

  • Your IPTV provider: your device connects directly to the server you entered, to load channels, films and series. We are not involved in that connection.
  • The Movie Database (TMDb): to fetch posters and metadata for films and series, the app may request information from TMDb. This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.

Content

Easy IPTV does not host, provide or supply any channels, playlists or media content. All content you see comes from the provider you configure.

Children

Easy IPTV plays unfiltered streams supplied by your provider and is rated for adults. It is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@gmbstudio.cc
