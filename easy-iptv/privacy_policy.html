Privacy Policy — Easy IPTV

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Easy IPTV is designed to respect your privacy. In short: we do not collect any data, and we do not host any content. Easy IPTV is a media player for an IPTV service that you provide yourself.

Data we collect

None. Easy IPTV has no user accounts and no login. We do not operate any servers that receive your data, and we do not use any analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs.

Data stored on your device

Everything the app remembers stays on your device — and, if you have iCloud enabled, in your own private iCloud key-value storage. None of it is ever sent to us. This includes:

  • The credentials and URLs of the IPTV provider(s) you add (Xtream Codes account or M3U playlist), used only to connect your device directly to the provider you configured.
  • Any program-guide (XMLTV/EPG) URL you add, or one your playlist declares, used to download the guide directly from that source.
  • Your preferences, favorites and “continue watching” history (which channels or titles you were watching and your position in them).
  • Your parental PIN, if you set one. It is stored on your device (and your private iCloud) and is never transmitted to us.

Services your device contacts

  • Your IPTV provider: your device connects directly to the server you entered, to load channels, films and series. We are not involved in that connection.
  • Your program guide (EPG): if you configure an XMLTV guide URL, or your playlist supplies one, your device downloads it directly from that source.
  • The Movie Database (TMDb): to fetch posters and metadata for films and series, the app may request information from TMDb. This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
  • fanart.tv: the app may request additional artwork (such as title logos) for films and series from fanart.tv.

These requests carry only what is needed to fetch artwork, metadata or the guide (for example a title identifier or the URL you configured). They do not include any account or identity information about you, because the app has none.

Content

Easy IPTV does not host, provide or supply any channels, playlists or media content. All content you see comes from the provider you configure.

Children

Easy IPTV plays unfiltered streams supplied by your provider and is rated for adults. It is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
