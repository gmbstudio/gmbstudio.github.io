Your IPTV, done right on Apple TV.

Easy IPTV is a fast, native player for Apple TV. Bring your own provider — an Xtream Codes account or an M3U playlist — and watch your live channels, films and series in a clean, focus-first interface built for the remote.

How it works

Easy IPTV is a player only. It ships with no channels and no content. You bring your own IPTV subscription — or a public, legal playlist — add it once in Settings, and start watching. We don't host, provide or link to any content.
