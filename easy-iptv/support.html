Getting started

Easy IPTV is a player — it comes with no channels. To watch anything you add your own provider:

  1. Open the side menu and go to Settings → Providers → Add provider.
  2. Choose Xtream Codes (host, username, password) or M3U playlist (paste the playlist URL).
  3. Save. The app syncs your catalogue. Open Live TV to start watching.

Frequently asked

Does the app include channels?
No. Easy IPTV does not host or provide any content. You need your own IPTV subscription, or a public legal playlist.

A channel won't play.
Easy IPTV automatically tries the next quality or source for that channel. If every source is down, the channel is from your provider — check that your subscription and the stream are active.

Where are my login details stored?
On your device (and your private iCloud, if enabled). They are never sent to us. See our Privacy Policy.

Which devices are supported?
Apple TV (tvOS). iPhone and iPad support is on the way.
