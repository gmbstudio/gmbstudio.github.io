Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Family Meal Planner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Recipes and Meal Plans: Recipe names, ingredients, preparation steps, and meal planning data you create within the app.
  • Shopping Lists: Shopping list items you generate or manually add.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for advertising purposes.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, feature usage patterns, and crash reports.
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID for personalized advertising (with your consent).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • To store your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists locally on your device
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyze app usage and improve our services
  • To process in-app purchases

3. Data Storage

Family Meal Planner is a local-first application. This means:

  • All your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists are stored locally on your device
  • Your personal content data is not uploaded to our servers
  • You have full control over your data

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy. This includes:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data for ad personalization

4.2 Google Play Services

For in-app purchases and app functionality. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements to support free access to features. You can:

  • Purchase the premium version to remove all advertisements
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Delete Data: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in device settings.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Adjust your device's advertising settings to limit ad tracking.
  • Ad-Free Experience: Purchase the premium version to remove advertisements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
